# Senn Parents — Brand Spec

**Status:** Working spec (no formal brand-development run)
**Created:** 2026-03-14

---

## Brand Name
**SENN PARENTS**
Parent Advisory Council, Nicholas Senn High School

## Brand Essence
Parent voice.

## Brand Personality
Organized warmth. Institutional enough to be taken seriously, warm enough to feel like community.

## Tagline
"Parent-led. For our kids."

---

## Color Palette

### Primary (from Senn High School)
- **Senn Green (primary):** `#2E7D32` — the school's core green, anchor color
- **Senn Dark Green:** `#1B5E20` — depth, headers, nav backgrounds
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` — clean space, text on dark

### PAC Accent (distinguishes parent identity from school admin)
- **Warm Gold:** `#D4A017` — buttons, highlights, CTAs. Signals "parents" not "institution"
- **Gold Light:** `#FDF3D7` — background tint for featured sections

### Neutral
- **Off-White:** `#F5F7F5` — section backgrounds with green warmth
- **Gray 600:** `#4B5563` — body text
- **Gray 900:** `#1A1A1A` — headlines, nav text on light backgrounds

---

## Typography

- **Display / Headlines:** Inter, 700-800 weight
- **Body:** Inter, 400-500 weight
- **Source:** Google Fonts (free, no licensing)
- **Fallback:** -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, sans-serif

---

## Logo System

### Pattern (follows Senn sub-brand convention)
All Senn sub-brands use: **"SENN" bold + sub-brand name**
- Senn Arts (gear icon)
- Senn Design (circle/lens icon)
- Senn Alumni (bulldog)
- Senn Bulldogs (bulldog)

### SENN PARENTS
- **Icon:** Shield — protection, advocacy, "we've got your back"
- **Wordmark:** "SENN" in bold + "PARENTS" in medium weight
- **Style:** Clean geometric shield, not ornate. Modern, accessible.
- **Color variations:** Green on white, white on green, gold accent version

---

## Voice

- Direct, warm, organized
- "All parents welcome" energy
- No jargon, no bureaucracy-speak
- Bilingual consideration (English + Spanish)
- Never sounds like admin. Always sounds like parents talking to parents.
