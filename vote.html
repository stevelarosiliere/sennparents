Help Us Decide What Happens First

The PAC has Title I funds to invest in parent workshops before the school year ends on June 4. Based on what you told us, here are four options. Pick the one you want most.

🎓

College & FAFSA Planning Night

Help your student prepare for college applications, financial aid, and career paths. A trained facilitator walks parents through the process step by step.

Mental Health & Wellness Workshop
