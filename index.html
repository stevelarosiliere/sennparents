About

What Is the PAC?

The Parent Advisory Council is a parent-led body at every Title I school. It exists to make sure parent voices shape how the school invests in family engagement.

💰

Budget Recommendations

We advise on how the school's Title I parent engagement budget is spent.

📝

Parent Involvement Policy

We co-develop the parent involvement policy with administration.

🎓

Workshops and Resources

We organize workshops and resources for families.

🤝

School-Parent Compact

We create the agreement between families and the school about shared responsibilities.

⚖️

Your Rights

We connect parents with information about their rights under federal education law.

The PAC is led by parents, for parents. We are independent of school administration.
